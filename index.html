Why the Deal Stalled — Paul's Admission on Record
LP equity dried up during the interest rate hike cycle (2022–2023) when the project was in its initial stages. Ivan and Paul's LP rolodex is thin — they were relying on broker-dealers and RIA channels but lacked deep relationships. Paul previously relied on Reef Capital's internal investment committee for LP raises and had never independently raised LP equity before this project. The debt has been easy to source. The gap has always been LP capital at the 35% equity layer. Mr. Dickson's response: "That is what we do every day."
