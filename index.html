Why the Deal Stalled — Paul & Ivan on Record
LP equity dried up during the 2022 to 2023 interest rate cycle. Ivan and Paul's LP rolodex is thin — they pivoted toward senior debt and gap financing and never developed deep LP relationships. Paul previously relied on Reef Capital's internal investment committee and had never independently raised LP equity before this project. Ivan acknowledged Southwell does not specialize in LP. No broker dealer or placement agent has been contracted. Paul summed it up: "Boy, I would love to unbootstrap it." Mr. Dickson's response: "That is what we do every day."
Note — Ivan's Bridge Loan Pivot Attempt on Call
Near the close of the call, Ivan attempted to reframe the $10M pref equity ask as a bridge loan to be taken out by townhome sale proceeds, positioning it as an alternative to EB-5 dependency. Mr. Dickson's response was direct: "If the EB-5 participation takes me out of the deal, I'm not comfortable infusing capital into it." Mr. Dickson made clear he does not want his exit contingent on any government process or third-party approval timeline. This reframe was not accepted and the one-pager requested by Mr. Dickson should reflect the deal as Paul originally described it without EB-5 as any component of the exit or repayment structure.
